# **App Name**: HealthFlow

## Core Features:

- Patient Sign-Up and Authentication: Allows patients to create accounts and securely log in to the platform, storing patient data.
- Employee Sign-Up and Authentication: Enables healthcare professionals to register and access their portal with appropriate credentials and role-based permissions, storing employee data.
- Appointment Booking: Patients can schedule appointments (hospital visit or online meeting) with available doctors; store booking information. Integration with Google Meet for video calls.
- Doctor Info Directory with AI doctor photos: Display doctor list with name, specialization and availability; doctor photos generated by AI. Helps patients find and select the appropriate healthcare provider.
- AI Chatbot Assistant: AI assistant to help navigate the site and answer common questions. An LLM tool is used to respond appropriately.
- Employee Online Attendance System: Doctors can check-in at start and check-out at end of shift; logging surgeries/appointments; attendance is recorded for administrative purposes.
- Hospital Reviews Section: Enables patients to view existing reviews and ratings of the hospital and add their own reviews, facilitating informed decision-making.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light, clean blue (#A0D2EB) to convey trust and health.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F8FF), almost white, to promote a sense of calm and cleanliness.
- Accent color: Soft teal (#77B5FE) to highlight interactive elements and important information.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font for clarity and modern appeal.
- Use simple, clear, and recognizable icons, such as those from the Material Design icon set, to improve usability.
- Maintain a clean, card-based layout to present information clearly and segment content effectively.
- Implement subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience without distracting from core content.
